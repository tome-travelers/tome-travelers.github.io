Tome Travelers - Battle


The percentage indicates the probability that the given attack is available in the battle. Click on an attack to equip/remove it. The Basic Attack is used to fill up the total probability to 100% and can't be removed by clicking. Your attacks can't exceed the total 100% probability. In the battle if there are multiple enemies click on the one you want to target with your attack. Then click the button with the name of the attack you want to perform. Hovering over a button shows the description of the attack.
